Responsible Vulnerability Disclosure Policy

T-Mat Global is committed to maintaining the security of our systems and services. We encourage responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities.

Report vulnerabilities to: security@t-matglobal.com

  • Acknowledgement within 48 hours
  • No legal action for good-faith research
  • Coordinated disclosure
